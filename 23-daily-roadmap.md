# 1000-Day Daily Learning Roadmap

## Overview

- **3 Years = ~1000 Days**
- **Each year** has ~333 days (accounting for breaks and buffer weeks)
- **Daily commitment**: 2-4 hours (weekdays), 4-6 hours (weekends)
- **Total estimated study hours**: ~3,500-5,000 hours
